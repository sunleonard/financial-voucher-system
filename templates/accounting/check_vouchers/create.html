What is a Check Voucher?

A Check Voucher (CV) records actual payments made to vendors, suppliers, or service providers. It represents money leaving your company.

Accounting Impact:
DEBIT
Payable ↓
Debt decreases
CREDIT
Cash ↓
Money out
Payment Workflow
  1. VP Created
    Payment obligation recorded
  2. CV Created
    Actual payment made (this form)
  3. VP Paid
    Obligation marked as satisfied
  4. Bank Updated
    Cash balance adjusted
Quick Tips
  • Link to VP: Select an existing VP to automatically fill details
  • Bank Account: Choose the correct bank account for the payment
  • Check Number: Optional but useful for tracking physical checks
  • Balance: Advanced entries should balance (debits = credits)

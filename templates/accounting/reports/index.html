Report Descriptions
Trial Balance
Lists all accounts with their debit and credit balances. Used to verify that the accounting equation (Assets = Liabilities + Equity) is in balance.
Chart of Accounts
Complete listing of all account codes and descriptions used in the accounting system.
Vouchers Payable (VP)
Lists all payment obligations to vendors and suppliers, showing amounts owed and due dates.
Check Vouchers (CV)
Lists all payments made to vendors, showing check numbers, dates, and amounts paid.
Balance Sheet
Shows the financial position at a specific date, listing assets, liabilities, and equity.
Income Statement
Shows revenue and expenses for a period, calculating net income or loss.
